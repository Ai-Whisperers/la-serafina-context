## What this repo is

Context-only research repo for a possible engagement with **La Serafina**, the flagship cultural project of **AIREANA** — a Paraguayan lesbian-feminist NGO founded in 2003 in Asunción.

## Read in this order

1. `README.md` — what La Serafina is, key data, areas of intersection with Ai-Whisperers.
2. `00_brief/sensitive-vertical-flag.md` — OPSEC + political risk. Read this **before** sending any external communication.
3. `01_organization/la-serafina-overview.md` — what La Serafina actually does day-to-day.
4. `05_website-audit/what-they-need.md` — the prioritized action stack (P0–P4).
5. `09_deliverables/brief-ivan.md` — one-page founder-language brief.
6. `09_deliverables/PACK_PARA_CLIENTE.md` — what we'd send to AIREANA if they engage.

## Three things to remember

1. **They are an LGBTIQ+ org in a politically-hostile country.** Apply `Ai-Whisperers/somosgay-context` OPSEC rules verbatim. Don't expose donors beyond what's already public.
2. **They have a working site already** (`aireana.org.py`). Audit it before proposing a rebuild — most of the work is making the existing one faster, more discoverable, and better-organized.
3. **Their superpower is cultural, not tech.** The bar is: "respect what they already do, just help them do more of it."
