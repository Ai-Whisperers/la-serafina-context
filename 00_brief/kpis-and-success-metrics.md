- Ro'hendu call volume (sensitive — handled by AIREANA's own staff)

## Anti-metrics (signals of over-reach)

- ❌ Number of files in this repo (depth ≠ quality)
- ❌ Speed of site build (correctness > speed)
- ❌ Number of pages scraped from aireana.org.py (respect their server)
- ❌ Public listing of anything AIREANA hasn't already published
