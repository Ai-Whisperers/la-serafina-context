# Client snapshot — AIREANA / La Serafina

> **TL;DR:** Paraguay's leading lesbian-feminist organization. Operator of La Serafina cultural space + Ro'hendu helpline. Founded 2003. Won the 2011 French Republic Human Rights Prize. Currently hostile political environment (Santiago Peña presidency, no marriage equality, no anti-discrimination law). Building a stronger digital presence is a major opportunity.

## The five Ws

**Who** — A 23-year-old lesbian-feminist organization. Run by a coordination team (mix of paid staff + long-term volunteers), co-led since at least 2003 by President **Carolina Robledo Desh**, with co-founder **Rosa Posa Guinea** as the long-tenured artistic/editorial voice. Other named figures: **Judith Grenno** (co-founder), **Chana Coronel** (Buen Pastor / visitas íntimas), **Paloma Vera** (Astraea Foundation connection).

**What** — Six integrated programs + the cultural space + the festival + the helpline + the publications:

1. **La Serafina** — cultural space + bar + venue hire. Eligio Ayala 907. Friday Culturales (weekly) + private events. Open since 2005.
2. **Festival Internacional de Cine LesBiGayTrans** — annual LGBTIQ+ film festival. **21 editions (2005-2025)**. Centro Cultural de España. 700+ films. 24K+ attendees cumulative.
3. **Ro'hendu** — "Te escuchamos" helpline. **Active since 2013**. 0800 110 108 + 0981 110 108 + WhatsApp. Mon-Fri 13:00-20:00. Confidential. Serves LGBTIQ+ community + families + allies.
4. **La Tatucada** — batucada (percussion group). Performs at Pride, Marcha 8M, Venir al Sur. Doble sentido: tatú = armadillo (animal) + vulva (jerga).
5. **Aireatena** — podcast. **12 episodes produced 2020-2021**. Spotify + Apple Podcasts + Anchor.
6. **Desobedientas** — youth LBT group 18-29. Cinema-debates, workshops, "meriendas informativas".
7. **Publicaciones** — "De toda la vida. Memorias de lesbianas en Paraguay" (2012 book), + audiovisual series (Lesbiatán 3 cap., Rohendu 3 cap., 17 años de historia, NEDTOLES 2), + Vulvasónicas ft. Aireana (3 songs).
8. **Incidencia política** — Codehupy co-authorship, Coalición TLGBI, RCTD, OEA, Mercosur LGBTI, Articulación Feminista del Paraguay.

**Where** — One physical hub in central Asunción (Eligio Ayala 907, microcentro). The cultural space is also a community gathering point. Ro'hendu runs nationally (helpline reachable from anywhere in PY).

**When** — Active since 2003. Major milestones:

- **2003** — Aireana founded (Feb). Co-founders: Rosa Posa Guinea, Carolina Robledo, Judith Grenno
- **2005** — La Serafina opens as sede operativa. 1st Festival LesBiGayTrans
- **2006** — Personería jurídica obtained
- **2007** — Co-founder of RCTD
- **2008** — Coalición LGBTTTI installed at 38° AG-OEA (Medellín)
- **2009** — Co-founder of Coalición TLGBI Paraguay
- **2011** — Premio Libertad, Igualdad, Fraternidad (French Republic). 15.000€. François Fillon in Paris
- **2012** — Viernes Culturales institutionalized. "De toda la vida" book
- **2013** — Ro'hendu launches. Chana Coronel visibiliza el caso de visitas íntimas en Buen Pastor
- **2014** — Code of Penal Execution submission (visitas íntimas LBT)
- **2016** — Aireana joins Codehupy
- **2017** — CEDAW Geneva hearing. Estado paraguayo evades the question
- **2020** — Aireatena podcast launches
- **2021** — "De toda la vida" republished + "Las conchas sin mar" concert
- **2022** — Ro'hendu atiende 1.013 consultas. Codehupy 2023: 76% discriminación en seno familiar
- **2025** — 21° Festival. Casa Serafina Dávalos restoration agreement signed
- **2026** — 1,400+ firmas collected for Casa Serafina Dávalos. IPS amenaza con rescindir el convenio

**Why we care** —

1. Massive gap between what Aireana *does* and what its *website* shows
2. Real public-health + community-care stakes (Ro'hendu, discrimination data)
3. Profile fits Ai-Whisperers' "sensitive Paraguayan verticals" portfolio (somosgay, dentist, sarah-lubricants, pierce-charm)
4. Existing public presence + international press footprint (Premio Francés 2011, OEA, Mercosur)
5. Direct donor relationships + 23-year track record = recurring engagement potential
6. Sister-org relationship with SOMOSGAY (already in our portfolio) makes engagement more efficient

## Sister-org relationship with SOMOSGAY

AIREANA (lesbian-specific) and SOMOSGAY (gay-men's health + advocacy) are **sister organizations**. They:

- Share coaliciones (Coalición TLGBI, Codehupy)
- Co-organize Marcha del Orgullo
- Both 23-year-old (2003 vs 2005/2009)
- Both Asunción-based
- Both international-network members
